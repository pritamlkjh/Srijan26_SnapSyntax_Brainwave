The Story Behind Studio S+

At Studio S+ Photography, we don't just take pictures - we craft visual stories that capture the essence of your most cherished moments. Founded in 2010 by Somnath Chandra, our studio has grown from a passionate solo endeavor to a team of dedicated visual storytellers.

What sets us apart is our commitment to capturing genuine emotions. We believe the best photographs aren't posed - they're the unguarded moments, the spontaneous laughter, the quiet glances that tell your unique story.

From intimate pre-wedding shoots to grand wedding celebrations and everything in between, we approach each project with fresh eyes and creative energy. Our philosophy is simple: when you're relaxed and enjoying your moment, we can capture the magic that makes your story worth telling.
